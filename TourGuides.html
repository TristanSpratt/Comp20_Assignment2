Our expertise

 

All our tour guides go through an extensive training program designed to strengthen their backwards walking skills, Zoom technical difficulties readiness, and general Tufts knowledge. As undergraduates, your tour guides will be able to give you insider perspectives on the Tufts campus life.

 
 

Covid Safety

 

In order to ensure the safety of all tour participants, we personally make sure that all our tour guides follow the COVID testing schedule they receive from Tufts University. Even though our tours are virtual, one can never be too sure...
